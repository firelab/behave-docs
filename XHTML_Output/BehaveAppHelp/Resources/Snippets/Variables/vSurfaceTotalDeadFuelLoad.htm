Total Dead Fuel Load

behaveplus:surface:output:wind-and-fuel:fuel:total-dead-fuel-load:help

Total Dead Fuel Load is the amount, by weight, of the total fuel load that is dead fuel (1-h, 10-h, 100-h, Dead Herbaceous). This calculation is made after the load transfer is done for dynamic fuel models, demonstrating the effect that changes in live herbaceous fuel moisture has on the model.
