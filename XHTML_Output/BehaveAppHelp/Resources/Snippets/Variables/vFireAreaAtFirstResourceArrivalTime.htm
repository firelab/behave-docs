Fire Area at First Resource Arrival Time

behaveplus:contain:output:fire:fire_size___at_resource_arrival_time:fire_area___at_resource_arrival_time:help

Fire Area at Resource Arrival Time is the predicted area of a fire when the first resource arrives and begins suppression. When the Resource Arrival Time is 0 hours, this area is the same as the Fire Size at Report.

Fire Area at Resource Arrival Time is calculated from the fire area (Fire Size at Report) and shape (Length-to-Width Ratio), the Surface Rate of Spread (heading), and Resource Arrival Time.
