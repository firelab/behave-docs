Elapsed Time

behaveplus:surface:input:size:elapsed_time:elapsed_time:help behaveplus:crown:size:elapsed-time:elapsed-time:help

When calculating fire size, Elapsed Time is the time from when a fire begins spreading at a constant rate (which may be different from the time of ignition) until the time that the size calculation is done.

In the Surface and Surface/Crown modules, the Rate of Spread is multiplied by Elapsed Time to calculate the Spread Distance. 

Elapsed Time is Used to Calculate

  • Fire Area

  • Fire Perimeter

  • Spread Distance
