Fire Size at Report

behaveplus:contain:input:fire:fire_size_at_report:fire_size_at_report:help

Fire Area at Report is the estimated area of a fire when it is first reported. For a prescribed fire, this is the estimated size of the slopover or spot fire outside the unit at the time it is detected by holding resources. For a wildfire, this is the estimated size of the fire when it is reported or when IA resources arrive at the scene.

Fire Area at Report is Used to Calculate

  • Fire Area at First Resource Arrival Time

  • Fire Perimeter at First Resource Arrival Time

  • Contain Status

  • Time from Report

  • Contained Area

  • Fireline Constructed

  • Number of Resources Used
