Total Dead Herbaceous Fuel Load

behaveplus:surface:output:wind-and-fuel:fuel:total-dead-herbaceous-fuel-load:help

Total Dead Herbaceous Fuel Load is the amount, by weight, of the Live Herbaceous Fuel Load that has been transferred to the Dead Herbaceous Fuel Load in dynamic fuel models. The amount of fuel load transferred between the two classes is a function of Live Herbaceous Fuel Moisture. The Total Dead Herbaceous Fuel Load retains the surface area-to-volume ratio of the Live Herbaceous Fuel while adopting the dead 1-h Fuel Moisture.
