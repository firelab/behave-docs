Probability of Mortality

behaveplus:mortality:output:tree_mortality:tree_mortality:probability_of_mortality:help

The Probability of Mortality is the likelihood that a tree will be killed by fire. The mortality equation used depends on the selected tree species. Behave automatically selects the equation in the Surface/Mortality module based on the best available science.

 

Refer to (https://www.frames.gov/behave/documentation-tips-history) for additional documentation about tree mortality in the Key Documents.
