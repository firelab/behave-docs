Fire Perimeter at First Resource Arrival Time

Fire Perimeter at Resource Arrival Time is the predicted perimeter of a fire when the first resource arrives and begins suppression.

Fire Perimeter at Resource Arrival Time is calculated from the fire area (Fire Area at Report) and shape (Length-to-Width Ratio), the Surface Rate of Spread (heading), and Resource Arrival Time.
