Probability of Ignition (0-100%) gives you an idea of how likely an ember is to start a fire based on site conditions. This tool only calculates Probability of Ignition from a firebrand or ember. Probability of Ignition is based on 1-h Fuel Moisture, Air Temperature, and Fuel Shading from the Sun.

Fuel Shading from the Sun includes shading from both clouds and overstory vegetation.
