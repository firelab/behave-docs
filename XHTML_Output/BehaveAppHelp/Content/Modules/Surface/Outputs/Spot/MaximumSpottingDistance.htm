Max Spot Dist from Burning Pile

behaveplus:surface:output:spot:maximum_spotting_distance:burning_pile:help

Maximum Spotting Distance from a Burning Pile is the maximum distance an ember is likely to travel and ignite a spot fire. Most embers land much closer to the pile.

Firebrand Height from a Burning Pile

Firebrand Height from a Burning Pile is the maximum height that a firebrand is lifted and is still viable. When burning under a canopy, it can help evaluate potential effects to residual trees.

Max Spot Dist from Wind-Driven Surface Fire

behaveplus:surface:output:spot:maximum_spotting_distance:wind_driven_surface_fire:help

Maximum Spotting Distance from a Wind-Driven Surface Fire is the maximum distance an ember is likely to travel and ignite a spot fire. Most embers land much closer to the fire front.

This model is only applicable in grasslands or areas with grass and short shrubs. A subset of fuel models are available when using this spotting model.
