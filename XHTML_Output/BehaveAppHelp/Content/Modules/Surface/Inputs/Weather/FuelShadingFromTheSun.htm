Fuel Shading from the Sun

Fuel Shading From the Sun refers to the portion of surface fuels not receiving direct sunlight due to a combination of overstory and cloud cover. It is usually estimated based on the canopy cover and forecast cloud cover.

  • Unshaded: less than 50% shading

  • Shaded: greater than 50% shading

Fuel Shading from the Sun is Used to Calculate

  • Probability of Ignition (Ignite tool, Surface, Surface/Mortality)
