Surface Spread Direction Mode

behaveplus:surface:input:directions_of_surface_spread__wind:surface_fire_wind__spread:surface-spread-direction-mode:surface-spread-direction-mode:help

Heading fire is the direction of maximum fire spread during flaming combustion based on slope and wind. 

Heading, Backing, Flanking option provides outputs for all spread directions.

  • Backing fire spread is 180 degrees from heading.

  • Flanking fire spread is 90 degrees from heading and is calculated at the widest part of the ellipse.

Direction of Interest is for a user-specified direction. The input is in degrees from upslope.
