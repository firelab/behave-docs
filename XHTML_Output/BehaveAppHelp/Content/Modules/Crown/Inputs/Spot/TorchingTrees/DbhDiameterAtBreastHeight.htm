DBH (Diameter at Breast Height)

behaveplus:mortality:input:fuelvegetation_overstory:dbh:dbh-diameter-at-breast-height:help behaveplus:crown:input:spotting:torching_trees:dbh_diameter_at_breast_height:dbh_diameter_at_breast_height:help

DBH, or Diameter at Breast Height, is the measurement of the diameter of a tree at about 4.5 feet (1.4 m) above the ground.

DBH is Used to Calculate

  • Bark Thickness

  • Maximum Spotting Distance from Torching Trees
