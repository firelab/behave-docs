Spotting Source Location

behaveplus:surface:input:spot:topography:spott_list_source_location:spott_list_source_location:help behaveplus:crown:input:spotting:topography:spotting_source_location:spotting_source_location:help

Spotting Source Location refers to location where firebrands originate on the terrain.

There are four options:

  • Ridge top (RT)

  • Midslope, windward side (MW)

  • Valley bottom (VB)

  • Midslope, leeward side (ML).

 

Spotting Source Location is Used to Calculate

  • Maximum Spotting Distance from Torching Trees

  • Maximum Spotting Distance from a Burning Pile

  • Maximum Spotting Distance from a Wind-Driven Surface Fire
