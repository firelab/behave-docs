Downwind Canopy Cover (SURFACE)

Downwind Canopy Cover is the percent of the ground covered by overstory canopy downwind of a spot fire source.

There are two options:

  • Open: Downwind canopy height will be divided in half before spotting distance is calculated.
  • Closed: Downwind canopy height is unchanged.
Downwind Canopy Cover is Used to Calculate
  • Maximum Spotting Distance from Torching Trees
  • Maximum Spotting Distance from a Burning Pile
  • Maximum Spotting Distance from a Wind-Driven Surface Fire
