The Rate of Spread is multiplied by Elapsed Time to calculate the Spread Distance of a surface fire or a crown fire. When calculating fire size, Elapsed Time is the time from when a fire begins spreading at a constant rate (which may be different from the time of ignition) until the time that the size calculation is done.

Elapsed Time is Used to Calculate

  • Fire Area

  • Fire Perimeter

  • Spread Distance

  • Fire Area at Report
