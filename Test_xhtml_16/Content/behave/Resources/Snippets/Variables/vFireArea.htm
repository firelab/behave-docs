Fire Area (SURFACE, CROWN)

Fire Area is the area of a surface fire that starts from a point source. It assumes the fire has an elliptical shape. Calculation of Fire Area is calculated from Rate of Spread, Elapsed Time, and wind speed. In SURFACE, Effective Wind Speed is used. In CROWN, 20-ft Wind Speed is used.
